Features

Built for TV and Mobile

One codebase powers TV remote navigation, mobile touch screens, streaming providers, Live TV, sync, and playback protection.

TV and Mobile Interfaces

Separate Compose navigation graphs provide D-pad-first Android TV and Fire TV screens plus touch-first phone and tablet layouts.

TMDB Discovery

Browse trending, popular, top-rated, genre, company, network, cast, season, episode, and recommendation data powered by TMDB.

Search and Curated Rows

Search movies and TV shows, then browse curated GitHub-hosted collections such as Oscar winners, classics, sitcoms, faith content, and action lists.

WebView and IPTV Playback

Dedicated WebView, IPTV, schedule, and YouTube player activities handle provider iframes, M3U streams, scheduled channels, and trailers.

Live TV and Schedule

Load M3U channels, XMLTV EPG data, categories, search, favorite channels, and scheduled events with channel chips that open the schedule player.

Watch History

Playback progress is saved locally, enriched with TMDB metadata, and surfaced in continue-watching rows for quick resume.

My List, Trakt and Firebase

Save movies, shows, companies, networks, casts, and favorite channels locally, then sync user data through Firebase and Trakt integrations.

Ads, Consent and Protection

UMP consent gates StartApp, AdMob, Wortise, and Unity ads while WebView ad blocking, popup suppression, and network diagnostics protect playback.

Current Implementation

A concise snapshot of the major features and systems currently implemented in KiduyuTV.

================================================================================ KIDUYU TV - VERSION 1.1.71 CURRENT IMPLEMENTATION SNAPSHOT ================================================================================ PLATFORM ================================================================================ - One Android app module with phone and tv product flavors - Android TV and Fire TV UI uses D-pad-first Compose navigation - Phone and tablet UI uses dedicated mobile Compose screens - MainActivity selects the correct navigation graph at runtime CONTENT AND DISCOVERY ================================================================================ - TMDB-powered home, movie, TV, search, detail, season, episode, cast, company, network, genre, and recommendation flows - GitHub-hosted curated lists for Oscar winners, classics, sitcoms, faith content, true stories, spy thrillers, action collections, and more - My List, continue watching, watch history, and metadata enrichment LIVE TV AND SCHEDULE ================================================================================ - M3U playlist loading and six-hour cache - XMLTV EPG loading and channel program matching - Live TV category browsing, search, playback, and favorite channels - Schedule tab with expandable events and selectable available channels - SchedulePlayerActivity fetches watch pages, exposes server switching, uses AdBlockerWebViewClient, and injects autoplay/unmute support PLAYBACK ================================================================================ - WebView player for movie and TV provider iframes - IPTV player for Live TV channels - Schedule player for scheduled channels - YouTube player activity for trailers/videos - TV cursor overlay for WebView navigation on TV and Fire TV devices - Watch progress updates and resume support SYNC AND ACCOUNTS ================================================================================ - Room database for saved media, watch history, cached rows, detail cache, and genres - Firebase Auth, Analytics, Realtime Database, Firestore dependency support, and Messaging dependency support - FirebaseSyncManager syncs My List, companies, networks, casts, watch history, favorite channels, and default provider - Trakt OAuth, token refresh, profile, history, collection, watchlist, recommendations, and scrobbling helpers ADS AND PROTECTION ================================================================================ - UMP consent gates ad SDK initialization - Core ad priority: StartApp -> AdMob -> Wortise -> Unity - Supports banners, interstitials, rewarded ads, StartApp splash helper, and Wortise app-open ads - WebView ad blocking, popup suppression, EasyList/EasyPrivacy assets, filter updates, and network-level diagnostics - Connectivity monitoring includes reachability, DNS, VPN, proxy, metered network, and network-state dialogs COMPATIBILITY ================================================================================ -
